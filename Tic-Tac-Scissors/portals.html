Portals!

Welcome to Portals! This game is similar to the 'Snakes and Ladders' game we all know and love from our childhoods with a slight sci-fi spin. The snakes and ladders have been replaced with teleporters that weren't calibrated properly! When you land on a portal you can be teleported to any other portal on the board so be careful!
